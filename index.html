✨ StoryMode

Ask the book anything
before you commit.

An AI chat for any book, in your language, streaming back in real time. Stay spoiler-free for the vibe, the comparisons, the Reddit consensus — or flip to Full Story and generate Story Cards: a swipeable, shareable recap deck anchored to the exact question that sparked it, saved to your Library for good.

  • Story Cards, on demand. Tap once to turn any part of the conversation into a clean recap deck — no separate wiki tab, no re-reading the whole chat.
  • Share the deck. Send a Story Cards set straight out of the app when you want a friend to catch up before book club.
  • Pick up where you left off. Every conversation persists per book — or start fresh with New Conversation whenever you want a clean slate.

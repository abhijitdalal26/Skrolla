01 For You Feed

Scroll covers the way
you scroll Reels.

Full-screen, one book per screen, an atmosphere built from the cover itself. No feeds to curate, no lists to build — just scroll. Our exploration-first algorithm learns your taste, but constantly works to expand it — introducing you to books you didn't know existed, rather than trapping you in a popularity bubble.

  • Cinematic by default. Every cover gets its own blurred, color-matched atmosphere — like Spotify does for album art.
  • Like, save, chat — right there on the feed, no context switch required.
  • Passive learning. Dwell time, likes, and chats quietly guide recommendations while preserving space for fresh exploration.
Onboarding

Seed your feed in three simple steps.

Tell us what you love reading. Select your genres and search for a few books you've loved. We'll start matching your taste instantly while keeping the rest of the literary world open for exploration.

02 Home

Curated when you
have no history yet.

Netflix-style rows instead of a search bar. Continue Exploring, Trending This Week, NYT Bestsellers, and Award Winners are seeded instantly from your onboarding picks so day one never feels empty.

  • Tap any cover on Home and it seeds your For You feed from that exact book.
  • “Continue Exploring” tracks what you dwelt on, similar to Netflix’s Continue Watching.
03 Search

A grid dedicated to
true discoverability.

Open Search and you're already scrolling. It displays a visually stunning, exploration-heavy grid showing the full breadth of our 70k+ catalog with no query required. Or search by vibe, like "dark academia" or "books like Dune," and let the AI interpret it.

  • Author search, title search, and vibe search are all unified in one bar.
  • The grid keeps exploring while you scroll, balancing what you know with what you might love next.
04 Browse

Every "best of" list,
actually worth opening.

Beyond the feed: dedicated shelves for Award Winners, Highly Rated, NYT Bestsellers, Manga, and any author's full catalog. Each is structured as a paginated 3-column cover grid that loads more as you scroll.

  • Goodreads Choice winners and NYT Bestsellers are badged and sourced, never just generic lists.
  • Tap any author from a book you like and browse their entire catalog in the same grid.
